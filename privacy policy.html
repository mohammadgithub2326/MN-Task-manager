Data Collection

Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when using our application, including:

  • Name and contact information
  • Account credentials
  • Profile information
  • Communication preferences

Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information when you access or use our application:

  • Task creation and management activities
  • Features and pages accessed
  • Time spent in the application
  • Actions performed within the application

Device Information

We collect information about the device you use to access our application:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system and version
  • Unique device identifiers
  • Mobile network information

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect information about your browsing activities and to remember your preferences:

  • Session cookies for authentication
  • Preference cookies to remember settings
  • Analytics cookies to understand usage patterns

Data Usage

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To notify you about changes to our application
  • To allow you to participate in interactive features
  • To provide customer support
  • To gather analysis or valuable information to improve our application

Service Improvement

We analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience:

  • Identifying popular features
  • Detecting performance issues
  • Optimizing user interface
  • Developing new features based on user behavior

Communication

We may use your information to contact you:

  • Service-related notifications and updates
  • Task reminders and alerts
  • Responding to your inquiries
  • Marketing communications (with your consent)

Analytics and Research

We use data for analytical purposes:

  • Understanding user demographics
  • Measuring application performance
  • Conducting research to improve services
  • Creating aggregated statistical data

Third-Party Sharing

Service Providers

We may share your information with third-party service providers:

  • Cloud storage providers
  • Analytics services
  • Customer support tools
  • Payment processors (if applicable)

These service providers are bound by contractual obligations to keep personal information confidential and use it only for the purposes for which we disclose it to them.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law:

  • To comply with a legal obligation
  • To protect and defend our rights or property
  • To prevent or investigate possible wrongdoing
  • To protect the personal safety of users or the public

Business Transfers

If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred as a business asset. We will notify you before your information is transferred and becomes subject to a different Privacy Policy.

Data Security

Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

  • Regular security assessments
  • Vulnerability scanning and patching
  • Secure development practices
  • Employee security training

Data Encryption

We use industry-standard encryption technologies:

  • TLS/SSL for data in transit
  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • Encrypted database backups
  • Secure key management

Access Controls

We restrict access to personal information:

  • Role-based access control
  • Multi-factor authentication
  • Principle of least privilege
  • Regular access reviews

User Rights

Access to Personal Data

You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you. We will provide this information in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.

Data Correction

You have the right to request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete information we hold about you. You can update most of your information directly through your account settings.

Data Deletion

You have the right to request the deletion of your personal information under certain conditions. You can delete your account and associated data through the application settings or by contacting us.

Data Portability

You have the right to receive your personal data in a structured format and to transmit that data to another controller without hindrance from us.

Withdrawing Consent

Where we rely on your consent to process your personal information, you have the right to withdraw your consent at any time. This will not affect the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal.

Data Retention

Storage Duration

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, including:

  • Providing our services to you
  • Complying with legal obligations
  • Resolving disputes
  • Enforcing our agreements

Deletion Policies

We implement the following deletion policies:

  • Account data: Deleted within 30 days of account closure
  • Task data: Retained until explicitly deleted by the user
  • Usage logs: Anonymized after 90 days
  • Backup data: Retained for up to 6 months

Backup Retention

For disaster recovery purposes, we maintain backups of our systems, which may contain your personal information. These backups are retained for a limited period and are securely deleted when no longer needed.
